Golden State Heritage

Pride of California

The Bear Flag

Symbol of Independence

The California state flag features a grizzly bear walking on a patch of green grass, with a single red star above it and a red stripe below. The flag, known as the "Bear Flag," has been California's official state symbol since 1911, rooted in the Bear Flag Revolt of 1846 when American settlers declared independence from Mexico.

The Grizzly Legacy

Monarch of the West

The California Grizzly Bear (Ursus arctos californicus) was once the most powerful predator on the Pacific coast. Known for their immense size and fearless nature, these bears were deeply revered by Native Americans and early settlers. The last California grizzly was spotted in 1924, but its legacy endures on the state flag as an eternal symbol of strength and resilience.

History

The Bear Flag Revolt: How California Became a Republic

In June 1846, a group of American settlers in Sonoma declared independence from Mexico and raised a homemade flag featuring a grizzly bear and a lone star. Though the California Republic lasted only 25 days before being absorbed into the United States during the Mexican-American War, the Bear Flag became an enduring symbol of Californian identity and self-determination. The flag's grizzly represents courage and strength — values that $BEARCOIN carries into the digital age.

🐻
Wildlife

The California Grizzly: Rise and Fall of an Icon

The California Grizzly Bear once roamed freely from the southern deserts to the northern forests. These magnificent creatures could weigh up to 2,000 pounds and were revered by the indigenous peoples for their power. Sadly, by 1924, hunting and habitat loss drove the California Grizzly to extinction. Today, it lives on as the centerpiece of the California state flag — a reminder of what was lost and a symbol of what must be protected. $BEARCOIN immortalizes the grizzly spirit on the blockchain forever.

Heritage

From Gold Rush to Crypto Rush: California's Pioneer Spirit

In 1848, James W. Marshall discovered gold at Sutter's Mill, sparking the California Gold Rush that brought over 300,000 prospectors to the West. This pioneering spirit transformed California into the economic powerhouse it is today. Now, a new rush is underway — the crypto revolution. Just as forty-niners sought fortune in the Sierra foothills, today's pioneers seek opportunity on the blockchain. $BEARCOIN bridges California's legendary past with its decentralized future.
